Yesterday we found a baby fawn sleeping
alone underneath a small playset in our
backyard. Does will intentionally "abandon"
their young for long periods of time so as not
to attract predators to their location. We
watched it rest for a while from the deck.

Later that night the mother came back. The
fawn nursed for a while, and then they walked
through our backyard out to the street before
disappearing into somebody else's yard.

This morning the sky was so clear, the sun so
bright that I didn't stop to ask if I deserved it.
Birds fed on a railing covered in seed, and I
watched them. Everything smiled.
